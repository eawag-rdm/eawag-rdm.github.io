Open Data Strategy

Eawag researchers want to publish Open Data

There seems to be a large demand at Eawag to make publication data packages publicly available. Right now.

Motivations

  • Increasing pressure to open up research data (funders, publishers, community).
  • Desire to demonstrate the high-quality of the research.
  • Increase citation count, increase chances for cooperation.
  • Increase visibility in the community.
  • Recognition that this can be a powerful career booster.

Eawag researchers want to publish Open Data

Change of plan

  • Aim to enable publishing Open Data now.
  • Exploit this demand to foster submissions to RDP:
  • Data organization for RDP similar to that required by external repositories.
  • Submit to RDP, indicate "Open Data".
  • Submission to external repository happens ~ automatically.
    (Currently manually by me).

Main foci 2017

  • Continuation of RDP software development.
  • Improvement of RDP reliability and data safety.
  • Production of guidance documents and documentation.
  • Awareness raising measures to foster RDP adoption:
  • Initiate community building, e.g. organize workshops with more general research data management topics.
  • Improve "Open Data Services".

Interaction with DORA

  • Users will always have to submit publication and data separately to two different systems.
  • The coupling between the systems is very loose.
  • Consistency will be checked by periodic background tasks.
  • Consistency will be enforced by auto-completion, alerting admins, reminder-emails to users.

Guidelines for Department Heads and Group Leaders

  1. Define the data that have to be long-term archived.
  2. Develop a metadata concept for all relevant data types.
  3. Establish a protocol that describes how data is submitted to the RDP.
  4. Mark for “restricted access storage” those data that legally can not be disseminated even inside Eawag.
  5. Define responsible persons for compiling, formatting, annotating and uploading the data.
  6. Inform all scientists in the department or group.

Time Line

April 2017:  RDP goes into production.

April 2017 - Dec 2017:
  • Publication data package submission becomes routine for a large part of Eawag.
  • The publishing of Open Research Data through the RDP increases.
  • RDP is leveraged to address general research data management needs in selected groups.


Nov 2017: Strategy Guidelines can be adopted.
